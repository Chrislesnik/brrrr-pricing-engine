---
title: Borrower Qualifications
description: Eligibility criteria for individuals and entities seeking investment property financing.
---

dscr.ai provides business-purpose loans to experienced and first-time real estate investors. All loans are made to entities, with personal guarantees required from principal members. Below are the key qualification criteria across our loan programs.

## Entity requirements

All borrowers must hold title through a U.S.-domiciled entity — LLC, LP, or corporation. If the borrower does not currently have an entity, one must be formed and vesting completed prior to or at closing.

<Note>
  Land trusts and living trusts are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Contact your account executive for eligibility details.
</Note>

## Guarantor requirements

Personal guarantees are required from all individuals with 20% or more ownership in the borrowing entity. Guarantors must meet the following criteria:

- U.S. citizen, permanent resident, or eligible foreign national
- Minimum credit score of 660 (higher scores unlock better pricing)
- No bankruptcies within 4 years (Chapter 7) or 2 years (Chapter 13)
- No foreclosures within 3 years
- No outstanding federal tax liens

## Experience requirements

Experience requirements vary by program:

| Program | Minimum Experience |
|---------|-------------------|
| DSCR | None — first-time investors eligible |
| Fix and Flip | 1 completed project (first-timers case-by-case) |
| Bridge | None for stabilized assets; 1 project for transitional |
| Ground-Up | 2 completed ground-up construction projects |

## Liquidity reserves

Borrowers must demonstrate post-closing liquidity reserves. For DSCR loans, a minimum of 6 months PITIA in liquid assets is required. Short-term programs require reserves sufficient to cover interest payments through the loan term.

---
title: Property Requirements
description: Eligible property types, condition standards, and appraisal guidelines.
---

All properties financed through dscr.ai must meet minimum eligibility and condition standards. These requirements ensure the collateral supports the loan and protects both the borrower and the capital provider.

## Eligible property types

- Single-family residences (attached and detached)
- 2-4 unit properties
- PUD and townhome properties
- Warrantable and non-warrantable condominiums (case-by-case)
- 5-8 unit multifamily (DSCR and bridge programs)

## Ineligible property types

- Vacant land (without approved construction plans)
- Commercial properties (office, retail, industrial)
- Mobile or manufactured homes
- Properties with significant environmental hazards
- Mixed-use where commercial exceeds 30% of total square footage

<Note>
  Rural properties with acreage over 10 acres require additional review and may be subject to adjusted LTV limits. Contact your account executive for guidance.
</Note>

## Condition standards

For DSCR and bridge loans, the property must be in habitable condition at closing — functional kitchen, bathroom, HVAC, and all major systems operational. For fix-and-flip and ground-up programs, the property must meet condition standards upon project completion.

## Appraisal requirements

A full interior/exterior appraisal by a state-licensed appraiser is required on all transactions. The appraisal must include a rental survey (Form 1007 or equivalent) for DSCR loans and an as-repaired or as-completed valuation for renovation and construction loans.

Submitting a complete file from the start is the fastest way to get to the closing table. Use the checklists below to ensure all required documentation is included with your loan submission.

<Note>
  Upload all documents directly through the dscr.ai platform. We accept PDF, JPG, and PNG formats. Please ensure all documents are legible and complete.
</Note>

## All programs — required documents

<Steps>
  <Step title="Entity documentation">
    Articles of organization/incorporation, operating agreement, EIN letter, and certificate of good standing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Guarantor identification">
    Government-issued photo ID for all guarantors. Foreign nationals must provide passport and visa documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Credit authorization">
    Signed credit authorization form for each guarantor (provided on the platform).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Property insurance">
    Evidence of hazard/dwelling insurance with lender named as mortgagee/loss payee. Builder's risk policy required for construction loans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Title">
    Preliminary title report or title commitment showing clear and insurable title.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## DSCR — additional documents

- Executed lease agreement(s) or market rent analysis
- Most recent 2 months bank statements (reserves verification)
- Real estate owned (REO) schedule

## Fix and Flip / Ground-Up — additional documents

- Purchase contract or settlement statement
- Detailed scope of work with line-item budget
- Contractor license and insurance certificates
- Approved plans and permits (ground-up only)
- Track record of completed projects

## Bridge — additional documents

- Current rent roll (if leased)
- Operating statements (12 months, if available)
- Payoff statement for existing debt (refinance)

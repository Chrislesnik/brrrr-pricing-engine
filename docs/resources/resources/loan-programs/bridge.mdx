---
title: Bridge Loans
description: Short-term financing for acquisitions, refinances, and transitional properties.
---

Bridge loans provide flexible short-term capital for investment properties that don't yet qualify for permanent financing. Common use cases include acquiring a property at auction, refinancing out of a maturing loan, or stabilizing a recently renovated asset before placing long-term debt.

## Program highlights

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Loan amounts | $100,000 – $5,000,000 |
| LTV | Up to 75% of as-is value |
| Term | 12 – 24 months |
| Property types | 1-4 unit, 5-8 unit multifamily, mixed-use (case-by-case) |
| Interest | Monthly interest-only payments |
| Prepayment | No prepayment penalty after 3 months |

<Note>
  Bridge loans are ideal for time-sensitive acquisitions where speed to close is critical. We can close in as few as 10 business days with a clean file.
</Note>

## Common scenarios

- **Auction purchases** — Fund the acquisition quickly, then refinance into a DSCR loan once the property is stabilized and leased.
- **Maturing debt** — Pay off an existing hard money or private loan to buy time for permanent financing.
- **Value-add repositioning** — Light rehab or lease-up before qualifying for a long-term loan based on cash flow.

## Transitioning to permanent financing

Most bridge borrowers exit into our DSCR program once the property is stabilized. We offer streamlined refinance processing for borrowers who originated their bridge loan with dscr.ai, including reduced documentation requirements and expedited timelines.

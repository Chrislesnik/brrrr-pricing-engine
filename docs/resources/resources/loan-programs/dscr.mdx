---
title: DSCR Loans
description: Long-term rental financing based on property cash flow, not personal income.
---

The DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) loan program is designed for investors purchasing or refinancing rental properties. Qualification is based on the property's ability to generate sufficient rental income to cover the mortgage payment — no personal income documentation or employment verification required.

## Program highlights

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Loan amounts | $75,000 – $3,000,000 |
| LTV | Up to 80% purchase / 80% rate-term / 75% cash-out |
| DSCR minimum | 0.75x (interest-only available at 1.00x+) |
| Property types | 1-4 unit residential, condos, townhomes, 5-8 unit multifamily |
| Loan terms | 30-year fixed, 5/6 ARM, 7/6 ARM, interest-only options |
| Prepayment | 5-4-3-2-1, 3-2-1, or no prepay penalty |

<Note>
  DSCR is calculated as **gross monthly rent ÷ PITIA**. When an appraisal includes a rental survey, we use the lesser of actual lease rent and the appraiser's market rent estimate.
</Note>

## Eligible borrowers

Loans are made to entities (LLC, LP, Corp) for business-purpose use. Individual borrowers must vest in an entity prior to or at closing. A personal guarantee from all members with 20%+ ownership is required.

## When to use this program

DSCR loans are ideal for investors building a long-term rental portfolio who want to qualify on property-level cash flow rather than personal tax returns. They work especially well for self-employed borrowers or investors with complex tax situations.

---
title: Fix and Flip Loans
description: Short-term financing for acquiring and renovating residential investment properties.
---

The fix-and-flip program provides short-term capital for investors who acquire distressed or value-add residential properties, renovate them, and sell for a profit. Funds cover both the acquisition and the renovation budget, disbursed through a draw schedule.

## Program highlights

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Loan amounts | $100,000 – $3,000,000 |
| LTV | Up to 90% of purchase / 100% of rehab / 70% of ARV |
| Term | 12 – 18 months, with extension options |
| Property types | 1-4 unit residential, condos, townhomes |
| Experience | Minimum 1 completed project (first-time investors case-by-case) |
| Draw schedule | Rehab funds held in escrow, released upon inspection |

<Note>
  Rehab budgets must be supported by a detailed scope of work with line-item costs. We review the SOW during underwriting and verify completion at each draw request.
</Note>

## How draws work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit draw request">
    Upload progress photos and your draw request form through the dscr.ai platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Inspection">
    A third-party inspector verifies completion of the work items included in the draw.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Funds released">
    Once the inspection report is approved, funds are wired within 1-2 business days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Exit strategies

Borrowers typically exit via sale of the renovated property or refinance into a DSCR loan if they choose to hold the asset as a rental. We require a clearly defined exit strategy at origination.

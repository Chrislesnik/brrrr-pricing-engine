---
title: Ground-Up Construction Loans
description: Financing for new residential construction projects from the ground up.
---

The ground-up construction program finances new-build residential investment properties from lot acquisition through certificate of occupancy. This product is suited for experienced builders and developers constructing spec homes, build-to-rent properties, or small multifamily projects.

## Program highlights

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Loan amounts | $150,000 – $5,000,000 |
| LTC | Up to 85% of total project cost |
| LTV | Up to 70% of completed value |
| Term | 12 – 24 months |
| Property types | SFR, 2-4 unit, townhome, 5-8 unit multifamily |
| Experience | Minimum 2 completed ground-up projects required |

<Note>
  Borrowers must provide approved plans and permits, a detailed construction budget, and a builder's risk insurance policy prior to closing.
</Note>

## Draw process

Construction funds are disbursed through a draw schedule aligned to project milestones. Each draw requires a third-party inspection confirming that the budgeted work has been completed to standard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Foundation">
    First draw released upon completion of foundation and framing inspection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rough-in">
    Second draw after mechanical rough-in (plumbing, electrical, HVAC) passes inspection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish">
    Final draw upon certificate of occupancy or equivalent municipal approval.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Exit strategies

Ground-up construction borrowers typically exit via sale of the completed property or refinance into long-term DSCR financing for build-to-rent projects. A clear and viable exit strategy must be documented at origination.

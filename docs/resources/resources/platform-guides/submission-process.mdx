Once you've priced a scenario and your borrower is ready to proceed, converting it into a deal submission takes just a few steps. This guide covers the full submission workflow from initial application through closing.

## Submission workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Convert scenario to deal">
    From your priced scenario, click **Submit Deal**. This locks the pricing and creates a deal record in the system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the application">
    Fill in borrower details, entity information, and property specifics. The platform will auto-populate fields from the scenario where possible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload documents">
    Upload all required documents per the [Documentation Checklist](/resources/underwriting/documentation-checklist). The platform shows you exactly which documents are still needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for review">
    Once all required fields and documents are complete, click **Submit for Underwriting**. You'll receive a confirmation email with your deal number.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After submission

Your deal moves through the following stages after submission:

| Stage | Typical Timeline | Description |
|-------|-----------------|-------------|
| Initial Review | 1-2 business days | File completeness check and preliminary underwriting |
| Underwriting | 3-5 business days | Full credit, property, and entity review |
| Conditional Approval | — | Approval with list of outstanding conditions |
| Clear to Close | 1-2 business days | All conditions satisfied, closing docs ordered |
| Closing | Borrower-scheduled | Title company coordinates signing and funding |

<Note>
  You can track your deal's status in real time from the **Deals** tab on the dashboard. The platform sends email notifications at each stage transition.
</Note>

## Tips for faster closings

- Submit a complete file upfront — incomplete packages are the number one cause of delays.
- Ensure the appraisal is ordered early. It's typically the longest lead-time item.
- Respond to condition requests within 24 hours to keep the deal moving.
- Use the platform's messaging feature to communicate directly with your underwriter.

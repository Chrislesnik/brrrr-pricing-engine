The dscr.ai platform lets you price scenarios, submit deals, and track loan progress in one place. This guide walks you through initial setup and your first pricing scenario.

## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Visit [pricingengine.pro](https://pricingengine.pro) and click **Get Started**. Enter your business email, name, and company information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Check your inbox for a verification link. Click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Add your NMLS ID (if applicable), state licenses, and company details. This information is required before submitting deals.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Broker approval typically takes 1-2 business days. You can price scenarios immediately while your account is under review.
</Note>

## Run your first scenario

Once your account is active, you can price a loan scenario in under 60 seconds:

1. Click **New Scenario** from the dashboard
2. Select the loan program (DSCR, fix-and-flip, bridge, or ground-up)
3. Enter the property address, estimated value, and loan amount
4. For DSCR loans, enter the expected monthly rent
5. Review the rate, terms, and estimated closing costs

The pricing engine will return real-time rate options based on the scenario parameters. You can adjust inputs and re-price as many times as needed before converting the scenario into a formal deal submission.

## Next steps

When you're ready to move forward with a deal, see the [Submission Process](/resources/platform-guides/submission-process) guide for step-by-step instructions on converting a scenario into a full loan application.

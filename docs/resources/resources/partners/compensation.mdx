dscr.ai offers a transparent and competitive compensation structure for broker partners. Compensation is built into the loan pricing and disclosed clearly on every rate sheet and loan estimate.

## How compensation works

Broker compensation is embedded in the interest rate via a yield spread premium (YSP) model. The rate sheet displays par rates, and brokers select a rate that includes their desired compensation. There are no separate origination fees charged by dscr.ai to the broker.

| Compensation Type | Details |
|-------------------|---------|
| Yield Spread Premium | Built into rate; broker selects rate tier |
| Broker Points | Up to 2 points borrower-paid (disclosed on LE) |
| Combined Maximum | Total compensation capped per applicable regulations |

<Note>
  All compensation must be disclosed on the Loan Estimate and Closing Disclosure. Compensation structures must comply with applicable state and federal regulations.
</Note>

## Rate sheet structure

The rate sheet is organized by loan program and displays pricing across multiple rate tiers. Each tier corresponds to a different YSP level. Brokers select the tier that reflects their desired compensation, and the platform automatically calculates the all-in rate for the borrower.

## Volume incentives

dscr.ai offers volume-based incentive programs for high-producing broker partners. These programs may include improved base pricing, reduced fees, or marketing co-investment. Contact your account executive for details on current incentive tiers and eligibility thresholds.

## Payment timing

Broker compensation is paid at closing via the settlement agent. Fees are wired per the instructions on the closing disclosure. There is no delay or net-funding period — you receive your compensation when the loan funds.

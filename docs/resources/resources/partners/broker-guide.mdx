dscr.ai works with mortgage brokers and correspondents nationwide to originate investment property loans. As a broker partner, you get access to competitive pricing, fast closings, and a modern platform that makes loan origination efficient.

## Why partner with dscr.ai

- **Competitive rates** — Institutional capital pricing passed through to the broker channel
- **Fast closings** — 10-15 business day average from submission to funding
- **Technology-first** — Price scenarios, submit deals, and track progress from a single platform
- **Dedicated support** — Assigned account executive and direct underwriter access
- **Transparent pricing** — No hidden fees or junk charges. What you see on the rate sheet is what you get.

## Getting approved

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply online">
    Complete the broker application at [pricingengine.pro](https://pricingengine.pro). You'll need your NMLS ID, state licenses, and company information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compliance review">
    Our team verifies your licenses, reviews your company background, and confirms eligibility.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Account activation">
    Once approved, you'll receive login credentials and access to the full platform, including live pricing and deal submission.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Broker approval typically takes 1-2 business days. You can price scenarios immediately while your application is under review.
</Note>

## Broker responsibilities

As a dscr.ai broker partner, you are responsible for:

- Collecting and verifying borrower documentation
- Ensuring the borrower understands the loan terms and business-purpose nature of the transaction
- Submitting complete and accurate loan files
- Maintaining active state licensing and NMLS registration
- Complying with all applicable federal and state regulations

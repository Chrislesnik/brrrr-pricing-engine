dscr.ai integrates AI capabilities throughout the platform to accelerate underwriting, document analysis, and decision-making.

## Document Intelligence

AI-powered document extraction automatically reads and parses uploaded documents like rent rolls, operating statements, and appraisals into structured data fields.

### Supported Document Types

- Rent rolls and lease schedules
- Operating statements (T-12, P&L)
- Appraisal reports
- Credit reports
- Insurance certificates

## AI Chat Assistant

The built-in chat assistant provides contextual help throughout the platform. Ask questions about your deals, get explanations of pricing calculations, or request summaries of borrower profiles.

## Scenario Analysis

Use AI-assisted scenario modeling to evaluate multiple pricing and structuring options for a deal, with automated sensitivity analysis on key variables like DSCR, LTV, and debt yield.

## Credit Report Analysis

Upload credit reports and receive AI-generated summaries highlighting key risk factors, score trends, and notable derogatory items across all borrowers and guarantors.

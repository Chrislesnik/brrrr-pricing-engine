Borrowers and entities form the relationship layer for every deal. A borrower can be an individual or an entity (LLC, corporation, trust) with one or more guarantors.

## Borrower Profiles

Each borrower profile includes personal information, credit history, and associated entities. Borrowers can be linked to multiple deals across your organization.

### Creating a Borrower

1. Navigate to **Borrowers** in the sidebar.
2. Click **New Borrower** and fill in the required fields.
3. Link the borrower to one or more entities if applicable.
4. Associate the borrower with a deal from the deal's detail page.

## Entity Structures

Entities represent the legal structures that hold properties. Configure ownership percentages, managing members, and guarantor relationships for each entity.

### Supported Entity Types

- LLC (Limited Liability Company)
- Corporation
- Trust
- Partnership
- Individual

## Guarantor Relationships

Link borrowers as guarantors to entities and deals. Guarantor information flows through to document generation and credit analysis automatically.
